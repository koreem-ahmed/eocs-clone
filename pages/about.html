Our mission

ABOUT EOCS

Bridging Egypt's rich scientific heritage with cutting-edge computational innovation for the next generation.

The Egyptian Olympiad in Computational Science (EOCS) challenges students to solve complex scientific problems using modern computational methods across physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics.

MATHEMATICAL MODELING

Differential equations, numerical methods, optimization algorithms, and statistical analysis

PHYSICS COMPUTATION

Quantum mechanics, particle physics, and astronomical simulations

CHEMICAL MODELING

Molecular dynamics, reaction kinetics, and drug design

BIOLOGICAL SYSTEMS

DNA analysis, protein folding, and ecosystem modeling
